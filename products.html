Machine Roomless Elevators

STS Elevator is counted amongst the most recognized names engaged in designing and developing Machine Roomless Elevator. Thought traction lifts were originally limted to high-rises, the innovative MRL design has allowed engineers to adapt elevators for low-rise build- ings and homes The advance of MRL in much appreciated by civil engineers, architects and users. These elevators save 25 percent electricity and 10 percent of construction area. MRL have broken through the prerequisite for machine rooms, providing limitless possibilities of creation for the limited space of modern construction. Features: Longer functional life, Required low maintenance
